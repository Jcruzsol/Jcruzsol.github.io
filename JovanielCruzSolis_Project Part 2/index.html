The Enchantment of the Seas

Come aboard one of the classics!

Originally debuting in 1997, the Enchantment of the Seas is one of the older models in Royal Caribbeans fleet. Quaint and charming, this ship was refurbished in 2005 and is perfectly suited for new passengers as well as experienced ones.
