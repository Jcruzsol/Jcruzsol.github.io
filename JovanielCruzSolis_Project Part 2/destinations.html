Where we went!

Costa Maya, Mexico

A little slice of paradise! You'll find a little bit of everything here, from ancient ruins like the Chacchoben to snorkeling and diving in the nearby coral reefs. With it's rich culture and beautiful landscape, Costa Maya has something for everyone.

Roatan Island, Honduras

Known far and wide for its beautiful white sand beaches, Roatan Island will feel like you're on a postcard. A paradise for thrill-seekers, Roatan Island has the worlds second largest coral reef, perfect for diving and swimming alongside exotic marine life.

Belize City, Belize

Belize, a melting pot for Caribbean and Central American culture and charm. Visiting the lush rainforests is a must for those looking to connect with nature. If you're feeling adventurous, try your hand at a zip-lining adventure or explore the Maya temples of Altun Ha or Xunantunich.

Cozumel, Mexico

An island paradise famous for its snorkeling and diving site, like the Palancar Reef, Cozumel is a beautiful mix of culture beautiful environments. Those looking for a relaxing time are going to find plenty of opportunity as well. With the many shops and restaurants at the port of call even the party people will have the time of their lives.
